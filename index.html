AutoCare MOT – Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

AutoCare MOT is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

AutoCare MOT does not collect, store, or process any personal data.

We do not collect:

  • Names
  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics
  • Payment information
  • MOT data or vehicle information

No data is transmitted to our servers or to any third parties.

2. Subscriptions and Payments

AutoCare MOT offers optional auto‑renewing subscriptions. All payments are processed securely by Apple’s App Store.

We do not have access to:

  • Your payment details
  • Your Apple ID
  • Your purchase history

Apple manages all billing, renewals, and cancellations.

3. Third‑Party Services

The app does not use:

  • Analytics tools
  • Advertising networks
  • Tracking technologies
  • Third‑party SDKs that collect data

No third‑party receives any information from your use of the app.

4. Children’s Privacy

Because AutoCare MOT does not collect any personal information, the app is safe for users of all ages, including children.

5. Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this Privacy Policy, we will update the “Last updated” date above. Any changes will be reflected in the in‑app Privacy Policy screen and on this page.
